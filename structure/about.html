About Us

Our Story

Etmanskie Contracting, founded in 2020, is a premier construction and contracting company nestled in the heart of the Ottawa Valley. With a strong foundation built on integrity, quality, and a relentless pursuit of excellence, we have rapidly emerged as a trusted name in the construction industry. At Etmanskie Contracting, we specialize in a broad range of projects, including residential, commercial, and industrial construction, ensuring each project is managed with the utmost precision and care from start to finish.

Our Commitment

Our commitment to quality, safety, and sustainability is at the core of everything we do. Etmanskie Contracting prides itself on its ability to bring innovative solutions to complex construction challenges, making us a partner of choice in the Ottawa Valley. We believe in fostering strong, lasting relationships with our clients, suppliers, and the communities we serve, contributing to their growth and prosperity. As we look towards the future, Etmanskie Contracting is dedicated to continuing our legacy of excellence, pushing the boundaries of landscaping to create spaces that inspire and endure for generations to come.
