Our Services

Lawn Care

Dive into the lush, vibrant greenery of a well-maintained lawn. Our lawn care services include meticulous mowing, edging, and fertilization to ensure your grass is healthy, thick, and beautifully green all year round. With pricing starting at $50 per visit, we treat each blade of grass with the care it deserves, promising a lawn that not only looks perfect but also enhances the overall health of your outdoor environment.

Property Care

From seasonal clean-ups to ongoing maintenance, our property care services are tailored to keep your outdoor areas pristine and welcoming. Services start at $150 per month, including spring planting, summer maintenance, autumn clean-up, or winter preparation. We take care of the details—mulching, weeding, pruning, and more—so you can enjoy the beauty of your outdoor spaces without the hassle.

Shrub Removal

Etmanskies Landscaping specializes in efficient and thorough shrub removal, ensuring your outdoor spaces are both beautiful and functional. Starting at $200 per project, our services include the safe and complete extraction of unwanted shrubs, root removal, and site clean-up. Whether you're looking to clear space for new plantings or simply want to maintain a tidy yard, we handle all aspects of the job with care and expertise, leaving your landscape refreshed and ready for its next transformation.
